Meet the Duckies

Bill

Originally from universe UDLR-BA, Bill was a scholar of the algorithms of the universe. One day, while reading ancient texts from a lost civilization, Bill found a lead to the most coveted ruin of them all: The Nexus of Infinity. Determined to unveil the secret of the universe, Bill ventured across planets to reach the ruin. Upon arrival, he was met with a massive structure that had intricate symbols. Bill noticed that some were glowing, and he hastily followed them. However, he overlooked the possibility of traps and fell into a deep hole. When he opened his eyes, he was unable to move and found himself at the desk, listening to a child ramble about nonsense. However, with no way of escape, Bill was forced to live in this new reality. 

Red + Pubert

Terrence and his brother red were world famous wrestlers who were able to knock down buildings with a single body slam. One day, an incident with their local gang forced them to flee their homes, and Terrence had to change his name to something much less conspicuous: Pubert. Now they are making a living by doing odd jobs such as helping burnt out cs kids with their code.
